Is this the same as the public demo?

The demo is the stock experience. Your purchase is the full source tree, docs, and license to use it in your own products (see LICENSE — not for reselling as another template).

Can I use Claude instead of OpenAI?

Yes. Set LLM_PROVIDER=anthropic and ANTHROPIC_API_KEY in .env.

Does MCP work on Vercel?

No — stdio MCP needs a persistent process. Run server/index.js on Railway, Render, Fly, or locally for MCP.
